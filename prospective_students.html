Joining my lab

I am always recruiting hard-working, curious students to join my lab. Please see below for specific instructions for applying.

Prospective PhD Students

Prospective PhD students must apply through the Vanderbilt application portal. In your research statement, mention you would like to work with me when describing your research ambitions. Positions in the lab are open subject to available funding. I am more likely to accept a new student if I can prepare before the application cycle. Please send me an email with the subject line "Prospective PhD student interested in working on ...". In your email, please make specific reference to your research interests and why you would like to work with me, along with an up-to-date CV.


Prospective Underaduate Students

Undergraduate students are welcome to join the lab and contribute to research projects. Research can be completed for course credit. Please send an email to me with teh subject line "Prospective undergraduate student researcher interested in working on ...". Please include a CV, a description of your research interests, and an overview of your relevant experience.
